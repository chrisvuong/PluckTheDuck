How to Play
🐤
Pluck Feathers

Click or tap on or near the duck to yank a white feather. Each one is worth +1 point. There's a short cooldown between plucks.

⚠️
Watch the Head!

Sometimes the duck swivels its head around to look back at you (a red ! appears). Stop clicking immediately — any reach toward the duck while it's watching gets you bit, and the run ends.

😡
Suspicious vs. Angry

The duck escalates: first it just watches (suspicious), then if it's still angry it gets red eyes + steam puffs. Sometimes it fakes you out and turns back without attacking. The higher your score, the less it fakes.

📊
Danger Meter

The bar at the top fills as you pluck rapidly. Past the white tick (50%) every additional pluck rolls a snap-bite chance — the duck whips around without warning. Slow down to let it cool back to green.

🌽
Eating Time

The duck sometimes stops to peck at the ground for a few seconds. You can still pluck, but if it spooks while eating it's 60% likely to skip the warning and snap straight to angry.

⭐
Golden Feather

Every pluck has a 2% chance of being golden — sparkles burst, score doubles, and the world freezes for a celebration popup. After you continue, the duck gets noticeably angrier and faster. Each golden stacks the difficulty.

🌙
Day & Night

A full cycle takes 5 minutes: the sun arcs across the sky, the moon rises, stars fade in. At night the duck gets sleepy: slower walking, longer reactions, more fake-outs, and roughly 30% less likely to skip-to-angry.

🌧️
Storm × 2

Random storms roll in (rain, wind, lightning, thunder). When the STORM × 2 badge is up, every pluck removes two feathers and gives you +2 score. Storms last 12–30 s.

🪨
Rolling Rocks

The duck wanders the ground freely. When it bumps into a rock, the rock rolls out of the way with momentum — purely cosmetic but cute.

⏱️
Timer

Tracks how long the current run has lasted. Pauses during the golden popup. Your best survival time is shown on the title screen when you re-enter your name.

🏆
Scoreboard & Users

Every player has their own record. Type your name on the title screen — returning players see their best score and time. The Scoreboard ranks all players by best score. After a bite, hit Change User to switch.

🔊
Audio

Click the speaker icon top-left to mute / unmute. Sounds: pluck pop, alert, angry quack, bite, thunder rumble.
